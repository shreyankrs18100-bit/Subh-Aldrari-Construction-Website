About Us

Company
Introduction

Subh Aldrari Contracting L.L.C. is a Dubai-based building contracting company providing integrated construction solutions for residential, commercial, and mixed-use developments. The company delivers civil, structural, MEP, and interior fit-out works under a single coordinated management system.

Established in 2024 and supported by an experienced technical and management team, the company brings a proven track record of over 160 completed projects across the UAE construction sector. Operations are carried out in full compliance with UAE legislation, authority regulations, and recognized international standards.

Overview

Company Snapshot

Subh Aldrari Contracting L.L.C. is a Dubai-based contracting company established in 2024, specializing in civil, MEP, and interior fit-out works. The company operates with a strong focus on controlled execution, quality workmanship, and strict compliance with UAE authority requirements.

Structured to deliver medium-scale projects efficiently, the company combines hands-on management with experienced technical professionals to ensure clear accountability, effective coordination, and dependable project delivery.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and leading contracting company in the United Arab Emirates, recognized for engineering excellence, strong safety standards, and consistent delivery of high-quality construction projects that comply fully with authority requirements.

Our Mission

To deliver construction projects with professionalism, precision, and accountability while strictly following UAE regulations, consultant specifications, and international best practices. We focus on timely delivery, effective cost control, transparent communication, and continuous improvement in quality and safety performance.

Structure

Management & Organizational Structure

Subh Aldrari Contracting L.L.C. operates under a structured management framework designed to ensure clarity of authority, accountability, and effective project execution. The organization is supported by general management, project management, engineering and technical teams, commercial and quantity surveying functions, procurement and contracts administration, site operations, health safety and environment management, and quality assurance and quality control systems.

This structure enables efficient decision-making and strong coordination across all project activities.
